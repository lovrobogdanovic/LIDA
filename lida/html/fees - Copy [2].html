Author No Shows

LIDA enforces a strict "no show" policy. LIDA 2016 requires that each accepted submission be presented by one of the authors in-person at the conference site according to the schedule published. Any paper accepted into the program, but not presented on-site will be withdrawn from the official program and proceedings.
