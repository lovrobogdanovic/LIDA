Workshop C

Guided Inquiry:  A Framework for Learning through the digital age school library

Carol Kuhlthau and Ross Todd

Short description:
This workshop is for school librarians who want to understand more about how to bring about the best learning opportunities for students in digital age schools through a guided inquiry framework. 
This workshop  will address:

  • What is guided inquiry
  • Philosophical foundations of inquiry learning
  • Research that supports inquiry learning
  • Designing and implementing inquiry learning in digital age schools
  • Developing working models of guided inquiry for sharing with other professionals
  • Developing instruction in using of digital and print information
  • Measuring the impact of guided inquiry learning
  • Using the results of learning impacts
  • Training school librarians and classroom teachers
