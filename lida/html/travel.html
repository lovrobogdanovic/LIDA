Travel information

Getting to Osijek


BY PLANE
1) Arriving to the Zračna luka Osijek (OSI): Croatia Airlines, TradeAir (flight schedule).
The Osijek airport is located along the regional road Osijek - Vukovar and it is situated about 20 km from the city of Osijek.
Transportation on the route Osijek - Osijek Airport – Osijek is quickly and accurately carried out by the OSJEČKI TAXI. In their own fleet, they have 26 Škoda Octavia cars and two vans for transporting a large number of passengers. Interested travelers are free to call 031 200 200 and organize their own transport.
Osijek can also be reached from the airport by bus (information at the main Bus station Osijek) or by rent-a-car service.
2) Arriving to the Zagreb Airport (ZAG) (flight schedule).
Transfer from the Zagreb airport to the City of Zagreb: additional information.
Osijek can be reached from Zagreb:

  • by bus (ticket purchase and timetable)
  • by train (ticket purchase and timetable)
  • rent-a-car.

3) Arriving to the Budapest Airport (BUD) (flight schedule)

Osijek can be reached from Budapest:

  • by train (ticket purchase and timetable)
  • rent-a-car

4) Arriving to the Belgrade Airport (BEG) (flight schedule)

  • by bus (ticket purchase and timetable)
  • rent-a-car

BY TRAIN
Besides Zagreb and Budapest, Osijek can be reached by train:

  • from Germany (ticket purchase and timetable)

BY BUS
Osijek can be reached by bus:

  • from Zagreb Main bus station (v. Marina Držića 4): ticket purchase and timetable
  • from abroad:
    • Flixbus
    • Croatia bus
    • Eurolines
    • Getbybus
